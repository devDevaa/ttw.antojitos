ABOUT US

Antojitos Restaurant is the brainchild of our great Founders. It is the result of their culinary life journey, a continuous voyage of inspiration, creativity, sharing and love.

Our compelling Sweets, Food and Beverage concept aims to combine the luxury of a life with the relaxed atmosphere of a restaurant.

1998

The beginnings in 1998 were modest, as ANTOJITOS was hidden at the far end of a narrow and bumpy lane. Finding the restaurant was almost like going on a treasure hunt.

Now A Day

ANTOJITOS is located in an heritage house of 1902. The restaurant allies Asian twist of flavors including new accents in modern European & Sweet fine dining.

Our Team

Antojitos will make the guests live an experience on its own. Antojitos offers more than what a restaurant can offer. Eating at Antojitos is a lifetime happening to remember.
